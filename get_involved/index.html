Get Involved with Princeton ACM

So you wanna get involved with us? Great — we'd love to have you on board!

If you’re a student:

The first step is to become a member by filling out this form. After that, see the world of opportunities for getting involved that opens up below! You’ll notice none of them require hard-and-fast qualifications or force you to go through an interview process. That’s because anyone, regardless of class year, major, opinion on Twilight, or anything else is always welcome. Click on a specific opportunity to learn more.
