ICPC: International Collegiate Programming Contest

What is ICPC?

The International Collegiate Programming Contest is an annual competitive programming competition for college students around the world. Teams of three represent their university and compete in a timed competition, where they are tasked with coding up algorithmic solutions to a variety of different problems. Teams who make it past the regional rounds are given the opportunity to compete in the ICPC World Finals.
