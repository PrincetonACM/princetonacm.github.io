ACM wins Collaborative Campus Challenge

Congratulations to all the students who came out to participate in the InterviewStreet Collaborative Campus Challenge. We took first place and won office hours with Jared Karim, one of the founders of YouTube!

If you're interested in joining us for the office hours, get in touch by email.

Princeton teams organized by ACM also took all top prizes at Google Games New York, held on April 21. Everyone who went had a great time!
