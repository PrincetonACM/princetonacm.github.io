Pearson Contest

Dear ACM Member,

I'm reaching out to all of the student ACM chapters to let everyone know that Pearson is sponsoring a Student Coding Contest this fall and we encourage you to participate!

The contest runs from September 3 - November 30 and is open to all undergraduate students in the United States. Students can work solo, or in teams of up to 5 members.

We're giving away great prizes too!

1st Prize: $10,000 and an invitation to present at SXSWedu

2nd Prize: $5,000

3rd Prize: $1,000

For contest details and to sign up, visit www.pearsonstudents.com/codingcontest.

Feel free to contact me directly with any questions at yez.alayan@pearson.com.
